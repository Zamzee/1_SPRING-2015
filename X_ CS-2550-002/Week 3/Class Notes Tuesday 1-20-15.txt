JavaScript
===========

- scripting language
- weakly typed
- every time a new page is loaded, any variables/functions etc are cleared from 	the previous page that sent you there (they will no longer be available)
- strings are immutable (cannot change them after defining it)

- when declaring a variable always use "var" tag, to make it local
